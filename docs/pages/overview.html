What is Domain Portfolio Manager?


Domain Portfolio Manager is a FREE domain sales and portfolio management software. It is written in PHP and uses MySQL for its database backend. Domain Portfolio manager can help domain owners better organize their portfolio and generate sales leads. Domain Portfolio Manager allows you to list your domains with their expiry, registrar, price, status, category, and more; it also allows visitors to your portfolio to contact you about a given domain, or to contact you in general such as for website problems or advertising.
